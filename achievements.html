v. Service & leadership

Beyond awards.

Recognition is one chapter — service to the community and field is another.

Mentor & Educator.

Co-mentored three Mayo Clinic trainees (GREP & SURF). Previously mentored nine Master's thesis projects, four summer fellows, and one international intern; led practical Immunology courses at the University of Hyderabad.

Conference Organizer.

Served on the organizing committee for two international scientific conferences, contributing to programming, logistics, and delegate experience.

Grant Author.

Key personnel on a submitted NIH R01 (Oct 2025). Author or co-author on fellowship applications to ADA, Breakthrough T1D, ALF, ASTCT, AST, and CCF.

Lab Builder.

Founding-member experience transforming a newly-sanctioned research setup into a fully-operational laboratory — establishing cell culture and flow cytometry facilities from the ground up.
